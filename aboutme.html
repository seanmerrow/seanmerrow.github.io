About Sean Merrow

As you know by now, my name is Sean Merrow. I live in New Hampshire and have been in the networking industry since 2000.

Employment

2016 to present - I currently work for Red Hat as a Principle NFV Partner Manager supporting service providers and communications equipment providers in their transition to NFV (network functions virtualization).

2006-2016 - During my ten years at Cisco, I supported several products and technologies. The first four years were spent on load balancers followed by a year supporting IP video surveillance. My last two years of support was spent as an escalation support engineer supporting Verizon Wireless's evolved packet core. I then transitioned from a support role to a technical marketing engineer/architect in the area of NFV working on virtualized packet core, GiLAN and OpenStack.

![Red Hat](https://seanmerrow.github.io/images/nortel-logo.gif) *2000-2006* - My six years at Nortel Networks were all in support.
